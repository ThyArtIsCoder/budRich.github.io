My name is Nils. I live in Sweden and I play the blues. I have worked the majority of my adult life as a CNC-operator/programmer in the steel industry. As all jobs it has it’s pros and cons.

I also made an attempt in the telecom industry, as a technical support agent for Lenovo.

But now I am looking for some other way to earn a living.

I have been very interested in computers ever since I got a C64 back in the 80’s. I have no formal computer related education, (except a year studying “multimedia design” at the millennium turn). I am also interested in information and communication.

I am going to create and publish content on these topics on this site, github and various writing hubs and communities. Maybe it can land me a job or at least some donations to keep me alive.

If you have some interesting mission for me, don’t hesitate to contact me, you can either post a comment directly on this page, make a shout in the gitter chatroom or send me a e-mail. If the mission is something like a smaller script and I am allowed to publish it, I do it for free or a beer if it is an interesting task.
